The Story Behind SHIMHAR

SHIMHAR was born from a simple idea — to revive the elegance of Ottoman-inspired furniture and adapt it for modern living spaces.

Our Philosophy

We believe in doing one thing and doing it right. Instead of offering dozens of products, SHIMHAR focuses exclusively on the Ottoman Muda — a timeless piece that blends comfort, function, and aesthetic harmony.

Why Only Ottoman Muda?

  • Focused craftsmanship and attention to detail
  • Better material selection and durability
  • No mass production or trend chasing
  • Designed to last for years, not seasons

Craft & Quality

Each SHIMHAR Ottoman Muda is made using premium materials and finished with care. From structural strength to surface detailing, every piece is quality-checked before it reaches your home.
